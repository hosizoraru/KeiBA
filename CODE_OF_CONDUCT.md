# Code of Conduct

KeiBA discussions should stay useful, respectful, and focused on improving
the project.

Expected behavior:

- Use welcoming and precise language.
- Keep feedback tied to code, design, data, or platform behavior.
- Respect different experience levels and device setups.
- Share screenshots, logs, and examples when they help the discussion.

Maintainers may hide, edit, or close comments and issues that derail the
project, expose private information, or target people instead of the work.
